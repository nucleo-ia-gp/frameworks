# Contributing to Núcleo Frameworks

Thank you for your interest in contributing to the Núcleo de IA em Gestão de Projetos frameworks repository.

## Publication Tracks

All outputs follow the Núcleo's IP Policy (3 tracks):

| Track | License | Approval | Examples |
|-------|---------|----------|----------|
| A (Open) | CC-BY 4.0 | GP notification | Articles, reviews, webinars |
| B (Framework) | CC-BY-SA 4.0 / MIT | GP + 1 chapter president | Original frameworks, methodologies |
| C (Restricted) | Proprietary | GP + president + DPO | PII data, algorithms, patents |

This repository hosts **Track B** outputs.

## Submission Process

1. **Proposal:** Open an issue describing the framework, its scope, and expected outputs
2. **Development:** Work within your tribe, following the standard deliverables cycle
3. **Review:** GP + at least 1 chapter president must approve before publication
4. **Publication:** Merge to main with proper attribution and license headers

## Attribution

All contributions must include:
- Author name(s) and affiliation
- Núcleo de IA em Gestão de Projetos attribution
- License header (CC-BY-SA 4.0 for docs, MIT for code)
